# 🏴 Insurgencies

## What are Insurgencies?

Insurgencies are revolutionary forces that sometimes take over your city when morale is low. There is a 50% chance of your city having insurgent attacks when it has a 25% [morale](morale.md), and it gradually gets lower until the chances get to 0% once your city hits above 35%. Cities commonly take two days to reach that mark, which means the insurgents could happen at a random time during the two days.

### Insurgent Ticks

Every game day, the server chooses a random time to do an insurgency tick. You do not know when this happens, and the tick happens at different times per game day.

For example, on day 1, the game could choose to do an insurgency tick at 2:58 PM, and on day 2, at 4:34 PM.&#x20;

The game could also tick on day 1 at 11:59 PM and day 2 at 12:00 AM.

> This information is useful as once insurgency ticks, it cannot tick again for the rest of the game day. If insurgencies ticked at 1:00 AM on day 1, then you know it cannot tick again until at least 12:00 AM on day 2. Meaning you can leave your cities and return by then to claim some provinces surrounding the city, as your morale is penalised by surrounding enemy provinces, making your morale go up faster.&#x20;

## How to take out Insurgencies?

> In Conflict of Nations, the standard protocol is to leave one infantry in every city where morale is low, as if the event of an insurgent uprising happens, you will be on defense, dealing more damage, and taking down the insurgents faster. However, there are sometimes where you don't have enough units to station on all of those cities. If an insurgent uprising happens, it will take over the city, and you should send 1-2 infantry to retrieve it immediately to prevent the number of insurgents in the city to increase.
