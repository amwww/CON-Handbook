## What is Conflict of Nations?

Conflict of Nations: World War 3 is a free-to-play, real-time grand strategy game where you command a modern nation in multiplayer campaigns lasting days to weeks. Compete against up to 100 human players by managing economy, diplomacy, unit production, and research across global maps.

Your primary goal is to conquer provinces and cities to earn Victory Points, with the first player or coalition reaching the limit declared the winner. Games emphasize long-term planning, as units move slowly in real-time, requiring foresight for battles, alliances, and resource trades. Key mechanics include three military doctrines (Western, European, Eastern), over 350 unit types from infantry to nukes, and terrain effects like stealth or radar.

Forming strong alliances and coalitions is essential in the modern military environment. Conflict of Nations: World War 3 provides you with the diplomatic tools necessary to form teams and interest groups to achieve joint victory and dominate the theaters of war.



* Real Time Combat System, which lets you take command of a modern battlefield
* Persistent Online Games, which last for days: Plan grand strategies and execute bold manoeuvres
* Hundreds of individually modelled units and modern weapon systems
* Terrifying weapons of mass destruction with lasting consequences
* Three military doctrines with distinct research trees and technologies
