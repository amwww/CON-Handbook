# 🥇 Gold

Winning is defined as surviving the game while one team reaches its victory goal. You do not have to be on the team that reaches its goal to be counted as "winning" the game.

After you win the game, every victory point you have is exchanged for gold.

Gold is a premium currency used for speeding up mobilization, research, and performing instant intelligence operations.&#x20;

One game where you are in the winning coalition usually yields around 1,200 VP / Gold, while a game where you survive usually yields 100 VP / Gold.

Speeding up research or mobilization by 12 hours takes 1,250 gold, so gold is still pretty scarce.

### Spending Gold

{% hint style="warning" %}
There is no going back or any warning before your gold gets spent! If you click the button, your gold is gone! Please think about whether it is necessary or you might regret it.
{% endhint %}

You should spend gold in emergencies.&#x20;

For example, if your city is getting invaded by 1 infantry and you have a mobilization on the way, you should gold at the last minute to finish the mobilization and defend your city.&#x20;
