### The Basics - Mobilization & Infrastructure

These are the things you should do at the start of **EVERY** CON game.

Efficiency is key. The main thing is **parrelel processing.**\
You want to maximize the number of things you are doing at the same time.

At the start of every game, you will always have enough resources to do the following things:

1. Army Base Level 1 on all cities - even if they are naval, you can't make naval units yet. We must take advantage of the time now to make infantry as ground forces are the most important early game.
2. Research Infantry - at the same time, go to the research tab and research motorized infantry
3. Mobilize Infantry on all cities
4. Build an arms industry level 1 on all cities. _(Some nations may not be able to do so.)_

> Some people like to build recruiting offices first. Although this is helpful, we won't run into manpower issues for at least the first mobilization round, taking around 18 in game hours, while arms industries builds in 9 hours.
{% endstep %}

### Invading Bots

For every game mode of CON, there are always bots. You can determine which nations are bots as they only have one city and a few provinces. Taking advantage of an early game, you should do your best to conquer the bots next to you. For less competitive game modes liek Battleground USA and Flashpoint, there are only one national guard per bot city, meaning 2 infantry should do the trick. However, for WW3, there are 2 basic infantry per city, meaning at least a recon division or artillery is needed.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Most people are inactive!

In many beginner modes, half of the server is inactive after a few days. This is great for picking your first target to attack. Try to invade nations with no buildings, as they have no new units, making them an easy target. If there are none near you, go after beginners (check rank) with only amateur builds like one army base, as they are only simple builds that cannot really build up a nation.
{% endstep %}

### Managing Research

Day 1 - Day 8

Normally, you usually only have enough rare materials for 2 research slots. However, you can buy more of them so you can research 3

1. (No Naval Cities) Go for Basic Infantry and Strike fighters- if you want to research one more, you can go recon OR wait for D2 for MBTS (Recommended)
2. (Have 1 or 2 Naval Cities) Go for Basic Infantry- Research corvettes and Strike fighters as well, OR research strike fighters, wait for D2 and D3 to research Frigates and MBTs
3. (All or Most Naval Cities) Go for Basic Infantry- Research corvettes and Strike or Recon- you should then progressively get stronger navy research (Destroyers, Cruisers)&#x20;
4. While you are researching new things, you should also progressively upgrade them **(Especially Motorized Infantry and other units that you have a lot of)**
{% endstep %}

### Intermediate Infrastructure

Pick one naval city and one air city. The air city should be your capital, which already has an air base level 1 with a fighter suqadron. The naval city can be anywhere you'd like that has coastal access. All coastal cities will have a Naval Base Level 1, or harbour.&#x20;

{% hint style="info" %}
Naval bases do give resource bonuses, so keep that in mind when picking your naval city.
{% endhint %}

Level up your air base to level 2, and naval base to level 2. Pick one heavy unit city for now, and level up your army base to level 2.

This infrastructure allows you to mobilise strike fighters, frigates, and tanks.

After your first round of infantry mobilization, you will run into manpower issues. Build cheap recruiting offices in at least 2 of your cities.

{% hint style="info" %}
Recruiting Offices reduce mobilization time and produce manpower.
{% endhint %}

Now, you'll <mark style="color:red;">definetly</mark> want arms industries of higher level for these cities producing these resources:

* Components
* Rare Materials

You'll <mark style="color:$warning;">problably</mark> want to upgrade these cities' arms industries once, as these resources you will run out of early game:

* Supplies
* Fuel

You <mark style="color:$success;">might</mark> want to upgrade this, depending on your situation. Typically you should do this based on your needs:

* Electronics
