# ⛳ Beginner Game Modes

After you sign up for an account, the game will automatically put you into one of the two tutorial game modes: Flashpoint & Battleground USA.

### Flashpoint

**Map Bounds:** All countries in Europe, the East Coast of the USA & Canada, the Northwest Middle East, the West Coast of Russia, and North Africa

### Battleground USA

**Map Bounds:** States in the USA, Districts of Mexico, and Countries in Panama

### Bots

Note that in these two game modes, each city in a bot nation (which has only one city) contains 2 national guards. You require at least 2 infantry to defeat them. 4 or more infantry is recommended, as you don't lose a unit.

But in other game modes, every bot city contains 2 infantry, where you require at least 3 infantry to defeat them. 5 or more infantry is recommended.
