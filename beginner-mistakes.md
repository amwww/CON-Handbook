# ❗ Beginner Mistakes

<details>

<summary>Only mobilizing &#x26; researching ground units, ignoring air or naval</summary>

This is also a common mistake. People tend to focus on ground units (specifically infantry) as they think it is more useful. However, only having infantry would cause your units to be destroyed by the opposing's naval and air forces.&#x20;

> Keep a balanced ratio, like 3 infantry to 1 air to 1 naval to 1 armored unit, making sure that you have a variety that can help you in different stages of the game.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Only claiming cities, causing slow expansion</summary>

This is a common mistake, as many beginners want to focus on destroying the country's cities, as they give the most resources. However, they ignore taking provinces, which can provide a lot of money and manpower, useful for buying trades that can quicken expansion.&#x20;

> You should send 1 infantry to go around taking provinces; this way, you can focus on taking cities while still getting the money and manpower you want, while being low risk.

</details>
